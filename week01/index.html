About Me

Hello, my name is Garikai. I am from Zimbabwe and I am passionate about empowering others through digital products and web development. I am currently studying Software Development at BYU-Idaho and excited to build impactful tools for Africa and beyond.

Harare, Zimbabwe

Zimbabwe is a country situated on the Southern part of Africa, which is South of the Sahara Desert. It shares borders with South Africa, Botswana, Mozambique and Zambia. The Country is known for its breathtaking landscapes, rich cultural heritage and resilient spirit. It is home to the Victoria Falls, one of the largest waterfalls in the world which locally is known as Mosi-oa-Tunya, The Smoke That Thunders.

The country boasts 16 official languages, including Shona, Ndebele and English, making it one of the most linguistically diverse nations globally. Its name comes from the ancient stone city of Great Zimbabwe, asymbol of African heritage and ingenuity.
