Site Plan

Site Name

Nungu Construction

This name was chosen because it reflects reliability, craftsmanship, and a strong foundation-values that are essential in the construction industry. It is short, memorable, and professional, making it effective for branding and recognition.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Nungu Construction website is to establish a professional online presence for the company, showcasing its expertise in construction, engineering, and project management. The site will serve as a central hub where potential clients, partners, and stakeholders can learn about the company's services, past projects, and values.

  • Company Overview - background, mission, and vision
  • Services Offered - residential, commercial, and ongoing projects
  • Portfolio/Projects - completed and ongoing projects
  • Contact Information - qoutes, consultations, partnerships
  • Client Testimonials - feedback from satisfied customers
  • News & Updates - announcements and community involvement

Target Audience

The site is designed for homeowners, businesses, and government organizations seeking reliable construction services. It also serves potential partners and investors who want to understand the company's capabilities and track record.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: I am a homeowner, looking to build a new house. What services does Nungu Construction provide for residential projects, and how can I request a quote?
  • Scenario 2: I represent a business that needs renovations for our office building. Can Nungu Construction handle commercial projects, and can I see examples of past work?

Color Schema

  • Dark Blue (#1E3A5F) - used for headings and navigation to represent trust and professionalism.
  • Orange (#F97316) - used for accent and highlights to symbolize energy and construction.
  • Light Gray (#f5f5f5)- used for the background to keep the site clean and readable.

Typography

  • Montserrat- used for headings to give a bold, modern look.
  • Open Sans- used for body text to ensure readability and professionalism.
  • Roboto Mono- used for special sections such as qoutes or technical highlists.
